Disclaimer

This application IS NOT REPRESENTATIVE OF, NOR AFFILIATED WITH, ANY ITALIAN GOVERNMENT ENTITY.

This app calculates the Italian tax code (Codice Fiscale) utilizing an algorithm provided by an external service; therefore, an active internet connection is required. Please note that the algorithm cannot resolve cases of "omocodia" (when two individuals share the same calculated code). In such instances, the calculated tax code may not correspond to your official one.

Remember that the only officially valid and 100% reliable tax identification code is the one issued directly by the Agenzia delle Entrate (Italian Revenue Agency).

Data Handling & Privacy

The data you enter and the tax codes calculated (the information visible on your cards) are stored securely on the Google Cloud environment linked to your account. This cloud synchronization is used EXCLUSIVELY to allow you to seamlessly access your saved data across multiple devices logged into the same account. Your stored data will NEVER be shared with third parties.

Document Scanning

When acquiring a new tax code via an identity document or health insurance card, you must take a photo that frames the entire front side of the document. This photo is never stored on your device or in the cloud. It is processed securely and ephemerally by Vertex AI. AI extraction algorithms can occasionally be imprecise due to factors like lighting, camera quality, or viewing angle. Always manually verify the extracted data before saving.
